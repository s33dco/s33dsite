A NodeJS news scraper command line package

Making use of the promised based HTTP client Axios and the browser emulator JSDOM BBCtopten heads over to the the BBC News website, identifies the ten most read stories, visits the links and delivers them to your terminal window.

There are no bells or whistles, simply the stories delivered to your screen. A great way to check the news, work best piped to less.

The code for this project is available on GitHub, the package available via npm.
